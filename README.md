# #4 Recipe Multiplier

**Stop doing kitchen math. Scale your recipes in one tap.**

Kitchens are chaotic. When you find a great recipe for 4 people but only need to cook for 2 (or 10), the last thing you want is to pull out a calculator with messy hands. **Recipe Multiplier** is a professional-grade minimalist tool designed for "Mama" in a panic and chefs in a hurry.

### ⚡ 1 Tool / 1 Action
- **Paste & Go**: Copy any recipe text and paste it. No manual line-by-line input.
- **Instant Scaling**: Tap x2, x3, or x0.5 to rewrite all quantities instantly.
- **Smart Numbers**: Specifically targets digits and fractions (e.g., 1/4 stays a fraction) to keep the recipe readable.
- **Privacy First**: No login, no tracking, browser-only. Your secret recipes stay yours.

### 🛠 How to Use
1. Paste your recipe into the box.
2. Select a preset multiplier or enter a custom ratio.
3. Watch the numbers update instantly. 
4. Cook without the headache.
